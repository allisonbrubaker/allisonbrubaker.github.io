This final project for my Session A Media Arts & Design class was a collaborative exploration of how fashion, coding, and digital fabrication can come together to push the boundaries of creative expression.

Printed Couture: 3D Printed Dress

Working with a partner, we designed a custom lace system using a code-based approach grounded in repetition, transformation, and recursion. Our goal was to generate intricate lace-like patterns that could be 3D printed and sewn directly into a dress, blending the delicate aesthetic of traditional lace with the sharp innovation of computational design.

Graphic Design in Illustrator on Hoodies

This project focuses on a piece I designed in Adobe Illustrator. These designs are all built from scratch using the pen tool, custom gradients, and hand-colored swatches. I focused on layering each element intentionally to bring depth and a lifelike quality to flat vector forms.

Ribbon Chandeliers

This project is a hanging installation I designed and built using circular metal rings and individually tied ribbon. It reflects my interest in texture, symmetry, and spatial design, blending craft and sculpture into a form that adds energy and depth to a living space.
